Publish a track

Fetch or paste lyrics, select a line, then pick a highlight color — a note card appears for that new line. Add annotation slip adds a card beside the line you highlighted most recently. Slips sit on the right; drag ⋮ to move a slip up or down, and use the color dots to match the highlight colors. Each slip has × to remove. Write a meaning; commenters use the meaning overlay. Pick at least one genre so it appears on the board.
