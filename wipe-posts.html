Wipe all community posts

This utility deletes every row the signed-in user is allowed to delete from the Supabase community_posts table, and clears your local copy in localStorage. If your RLS policy only lets users delete their own rows, posts owned by other accounts will stay until you run a SQL DELETE / TRUNCATE in the Supabase dashboard.

You must be signed in to your Noteion account in this browser first — open the profile page, sign in, then come back here.
